      How to join the Montgomery Lab.

      The lab combines experimental and computational genomics and looks for people who want to work with or generate novel genomic data and carry rigorous downstream analysis forward.

      At a glance

      • Postdocs should send a cover letter, CV, project interests, and two references
      • Stanford rotation students should send a short introductory note and a brief CV
      • Stanford undergraduates should send a short introduction plus an unofficial transcript
