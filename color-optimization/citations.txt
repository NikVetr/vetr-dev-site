Color Vision Deficiency (CVD) Simulation References

Machado, Oliveira, & Fernandes (2009)
- “A Physiologically-based Model for Simulation of Color Vision Deficiency”
- IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics
- DOI: 10.1109/TVCG.2009.113
- Supplementary matrices (sRGB, severities 0..100):
  http://www.inf.ufrgs.br/~oliveira/pubs_files/CVD_Simulation/CVD_Simulation.html

Brettel, Viénot, & Mollon (1997) (planned for future tritan implementation)
- “Computerized simulation of color appearance for dichromats”
- JOSA A

Implementation note / provenance
- The embedded Machado 2009 matrix table in `core/cvdMachado.js` is copied (reformatted) from:
  https://github.com/njsmith/colorspacious/blob/master/colorspacious/cvd.py
  (colorspacious is BSD-licensed; see that project’s LICENSE.txt for details)


Additional Color / Distance Model References

CIE L*u*v* (CIELUV)
- CIE 1976 L*u*v* colour space (CIELUV), defined in terms of CIE XYZ and u'v'.

JzAzBz
- Safdar, M., Cui, G., Kim, Y. J., & Luo, M. R. (2017)
  “Perceptually uniform color space for image signals including high dynamic range and wide gamut”
  (JzAzBz)
- Constants and matrices in `core/colorSpaces.js` follow common published implementations.

CAM02-UCS
- Luo, M. R., Cui, G., & Li, C. (2006)
  “Uniform colour spaces based on CIECAM02 colour appearance model”
  (CAM02-UCS / J′a′b′ mapping with coefficients c1=0.007, c2=0.0228)

CAM16-UCS
- Li, C., Li, Z., Wang, Z., Xu, Y., Luo, M. R., Cui, G., et al. (2017)
  “Comprehensive color solutions: CAM16, CAT16, and CAM16-UCS”

ICtCp + ΔE_ITP
- Dolby (2016) “What is ICtCp?” (ICtCp encoding)
- ITU-R BT.2100 (ICtCp usage for HDR/WCG)
- ITU-R BT.2124 (ΔE_ITP definition; ΔE_ITP = 720 * sqrt((ΔI)^2 + (0.5ΔT)^2 + (ΔP)^2))

Implementation note / provenance
- ICtCp matrices in `core/ictcp.js` and CAT16 matrix in `core/camUcs.js` match the values used by:
  https://github.com/colour-science/colour (BSD-3-Clause)
